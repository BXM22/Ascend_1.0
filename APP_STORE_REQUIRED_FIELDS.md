## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Choose a Build ‚úÖ

1. In App Store Connect, go to your app ‚Üí **App Store** tab
2. Select version **1.0** (or your current version)
3. Scroll to **"Build"** section
4. Click **"+ Build"** or **"Select a build before you submit your app"**
5. Select your uploaded build from the list
6. Click **"Done"**

**Note:** If you don't see any builds, you need to:
- Archive and upload from Xcode first
- Wait for processing (10-30 minutes)
- Then come back and select it

---

### 2. Complete Contact Information üìû

1. In App Store Connect, go to **App Information** (left sidebar)
2. Scroll to **"Contact Information"** section
3. Fill in:
   - **First Name:** Your first name
   - **Last Name:** Your last name
   - **Phone Number:** Your phone number
   - **Email:** Your email address
4. Click **"Save"**

---

### 3. Select Primary Category üè∑Ô∏è

1. In **App Information** page
2. Scroll to **"Category"** section
3. **Primary Category:** Select one (e.g., "Health & Fitness" or "Lifestyle")
4. **Secondary Category:** (Optional) Select another if relevant
5. Click **"Save"**

**Recommended for Ascend:**
- Primary: **Health & Fitness**
- Secondary: **Lifestyle** (optional)

---

### 4. Enter Privacy Policy URL üîí

1. In **App Information** page
2. Scroll to **"App Privacy"** section
3. Click **"App Privacy"** link (or go to **App Privacy** in left sidebar)
4. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Edit"**
5. Answer the questions about data collection:
   - Most apps: Select **"No, we do not collect data from this app"**
   - If you collect data: Answer the questions about what you collect
6. **Privacy Policy URL:** Enter a URL to your privacy policy
   - Can be a simple page on your website
   - Or use a privacy policy generator (many free ones online)
   - Example: `https://yourwebsite.com/privacy` or `https://yourwebsite.com/privacy-policy`

**Quick Privacy Policy Options:**
- **Free Generators:**
  - https://www.freeprivacypolicy.com/
  - https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/
  - https://www.privacypolicies.com/
- **Simple Template:** If your app doesn't collect data, you can create a simple page saying:
  ```
  Privacy Policy for Ascend
  
  We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of this app.
  
  All data is stored locally on your device.
  ```

---

### 5. Complete Age Rating üéØ

1. In **App Information** page
2. Scroll to **"Age Rating"** section
3. Click **"Edit"** or **"Age Rating"** link
4. Answer the questions about your app's content:
   - **Unrestricted Web Access:** Usually "No"
   - **Cartoon or Fantasy Violence:** Usually "None" or "Infrequent/Mild"
   - **Realistic Violence:** Usually "None"
   - **Profanity or Crude Humor:** Usually "None"
   - **Sexual Content or Nudity:** Usually "None"
   - **Alcohol, Tobacco, or Drug Use:** Usually "None"
   - **Mature/Suggestive Themes:** Usually "None"
   - **Gambling and Contests:** Usually "No"
   - **Horror/Fear Themes:** Usually "None"
   - **Medical/Treatment Information:** Usually "None" (unless your app provides medical advice)
   - **Contests:** Usually "No"
5. For each category, select the **frequency level** (usually "None" or "Infrequent/Mild")
6. Click **"Save"**

**For Ascend (Fitness App):**
- Most categories: **"None"**
- Medical/Treatment Information: **"None"** (unless you provide medical advice)
- This should result in a **4+** rating

---

### 6. Add Support URL (English) üåê

1. In **App Store** tab ‚Üí Select version **1.0**
2. Scroll to **"App Information"** section (or go to left sidebar ‚Üí **App Information**)
3. Find **"Support URL"** field
4. Enter a URL:
   - Can be your website
   - Or a support page
   - Or even a simple landing page
   - Example: `https://yourwebsite.com/support` or `https://yourwebsite.com`

**Quick Options:**
- Use your personal website
- Create a simple GitHub Pages site
- Use a free hosting service

---

### 7. Add Keywords (English) üîë

1. In **App Store** tab ‚Üí Select version **1.0**
2. Scroll to **"App Information"** section
3. Find **"Keywords"** field (under localization for English (U.S.))
4. Enter up to 100 characters of keywords, comma-separated
5. **No spaces after commas!**

**Example Keywords for Ascend:**
```
workout,fitness,calisthenics,training,exercise,strength,bodyweight,muscle,pr,progress
```

**Tips:**
- Use relevant keywords people might search for
- No spaces after commas
- Maximum 100 characters total
- Think about what users would search for

---

### 8. Add Description (English) üìù

1. In **App Store** tab ‚Üí Select version **1.0**
2. Scroll to **"App Information"** section
3. Find **"Description"** field (under localization for English (U.S.))
4. Write a description of your app (up to 4000 characters)
5. **First sentence is crucial** - it appears in search results!

**Example Description for Ascend:**

## ‚úÖ After Completing All Fields

1. **Review Everything:**
   - Go back to **App Store** tab
   - Check that all errors are gone
   - All sections should show green checkmarks

3. **Wait for Review:**

   - You'll get email notifications about status

```html
</html>
```

Host this on any free hosting service (GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.) and use that URL.
